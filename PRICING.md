## FAQ

**Q: Can I self-host?**  
A: Yes — all plans except Sovereign run on your own infrastructure. Sovereign includes source license for on-prem deployment.

**Q: What counts as an "office"?**  
A: Each distinct node in your federated mesh — a datacenter, region, or logical trust boundary.

**Q: Is there a free trial for paid plans?**  
A: Yes — Forge and Dominion have a 14-day free trial, no credit card required.

**Q: What Python version is required?**  
A: Python 3.10+. Zero external dependencies.
